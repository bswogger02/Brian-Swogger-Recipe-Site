Buttermilk Biscuits Recipe

View Recipe List

Ingredients

  • 15oz all purpose flour
  • 2tbsp sugar
  • 4tsp baking powder
  • 1/2tsp baking soda
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 2 sticks frozen butter
  • 1 1/4 cups buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl
  2. Grate frozen butter
  3. Mix frozen butter with the dry ingredients until thoroughly mixed
  4. Add buttermilk and mix until a wet dough forms
  5. Turn dough out onto a well floured work space
  6. Mix dough into a rectangle and roll out into about a 16 by 9 inch rectangle
  7. Fold dough in thirds and then roll back out
  8. Repeat until the dough is no longer wet or about 3-5 times
  9. Roll dough out into a rectangle about 1/2 inch thick and use a knife to cut out square biscuits, or use a biscuit cutter to create biscuits
  10. Place biscuits on a parchment-line baking tray
  11. Bake at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 20-25 minutes
