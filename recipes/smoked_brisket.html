Smoked Brisket Recipe

View Recipe List

Ingredients

  • Brisket
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Chili Powder

Instructions

  1. Make rub with suggested spices with whatever measurements your heart desires
  2. Put rub on brisket
  3. Start up the smoker and once the smoker has reached 225 degrees fahrenheit, put the brikset on the grill
  4. Keep adding wood to the grill and keep the smoker between 225-275 degrees fahrenheit until the brisket reaches an internal temp of 160 degreees
  5. Once the brisket has reached an internal temp of 160 degrees, wrap in foil or butcher paper and continue to cook until 200-205 degrees
  6. Once the brisket has reached the final temp, pull off the smoker and place in a cooler or oven to rest for at least an hour, then slice and serve
