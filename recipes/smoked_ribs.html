Smoked Ribs Recipe

View Recipe List

Ingredients

  • Killer Hogs The BBQ Rub
  • Brown Sugar
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Apple Juice
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Honey
  • Ribs

Instructions

  1. Remove the silver skin from the bones of the ribs using a butter knife and paper towel
  2. Add rub to the ribs fully covering all of the meat
  3. Once the smoker has reached 225-250 degrees fahrenheit add the ribs to the smoker and smoke for 23 hours or until the ribs have reached 16 dgrees
  4. Wrap the ribs in foil and cook the ribs for another 2 hours or until the ribs have reached an internal temp of 200 degrees fahrenheit
  5. Glaze the ribs in a mixture of BBQ and honey and cook for another 30 minutes on the smoker
